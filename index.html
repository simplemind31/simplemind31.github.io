Who's Lin Ming?

I'm Lin Ming Ou Xu, a 16-year-old student from Sucre, Bolivia, passionate about problem solving, technology, and continuous self-improvement. I enjoy understanding how things work, whether it's a mathematical idea, an algorithm, or the logic behind everyday systems.

I began with Math Olympiads, where I learned patience and creative thinking. Later, I discovered Informatics and competitive programming, where ideas are not only imagined but built through code. That combination of logic and creation made Computer Science feel like home.

Outside of competitions, I consider myself a cheerful and reflective person. I value teamwork, discipline, ambition, and meaningful friendships. For me, problem solving is not just about contests, it is a mindset.

My Experiences

  • National Competitions & Training Camps: Intensive preparation, selection processes, and teamwork.
  • Academic Programs: Member of Talentos Extraordinarios and graduated from CBA.
